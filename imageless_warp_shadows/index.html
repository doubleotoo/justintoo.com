Shadow Box Demo

Fancy drop-shadows using just CSS3.

  • Very easy to implement into your design.
  • Works on all browsers that can display regular css3 drop shadows and rounded corners
  • Degrades gracefully - other browsers simply won't display shadow.
  • Scales perfectly to any size of element.
  • Apply to a parent div or directly to an element (img not included)
  • Easy to modify to fit your needs.

The class applied directly to a paragraph tag.

Same style with fixed width and rounded edges. The same basic technique can easily be adapted to put the shadow on any side or to put a curved shadow on just one corner. Can work on any solid color background. Patterns, images, or gradients as backgrounds may not work.
